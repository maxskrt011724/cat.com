About Us

Welcome to PurPaws.Net! We're a group of passionate animal lovers who believe every cat deserves a loving home. Our mission is simple: to give stray and abandoned cats a second chance at life by finding them their forever families. Whether it's a playful kitten or a wise senior cat, we believe every cat has a unique personality and a story worth sharing. We started PurPaws.Net because we saw how many cats were in need of love, care, and attention. We wanted to create a place where these cats could feel safe, heal, and eventually find their way into homes that would cherish them forever. Over the years, we've helped countless cats, each with their own special journey, and it fills us with joy to see them thrive in loving homes. We know that adopting a cat is a big decision. It’s about welcoming a new family member into your life, and we’re here to make that process as smooth and heartwarming as possible. From helping you choose the cat that’s right for you to offering advice and support even after you’ve adopted, we’ll be with you every step of the way. At PurPaws.Net, we don't just want to find homes for cats – we want to build connections. Our team works hard to provide a safe, nurturing environment where cats can feel loved and cared for while they wait for their new families. We also believe in educating others about the importance of responsible pet ownership and animal welfare, so that more cats can find the kind of care they deserve. We’re not just an adoption center – we’re a family of people who are deeply committed to making the world a kinder place for cats. And with your help, we can continue giving these amazing animals the lives they deserve. Thank you for being part of this journey with us!
